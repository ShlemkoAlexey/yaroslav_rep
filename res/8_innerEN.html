Services

Working with large organizations and private individuals POTOKI GROUP provides the following services:
1. Cutting of galvanized sheet metal 0,3 mm -2 mm into strips.
2. Cutting of colored and galvanized metal into a sheet of any length given.
3. Rolling of profiles of custom configuration.
Availability of diversified equipment allows us to carry out custom orders of various complexity and extent. Our production is serviced by skilled professionals thanks to which we can guarantee the high quality of the finished products. We have optimized the supply of raw materials, so we set low prices for cutting and rolling. All the information on cost and completion period of your order you can get by sending an application by e-mail or by contacting our managers directly. Our specialists will consult you on all technical questions and will offer competitive prices on an individual basis

A wide range is one of Potoki Group advantages. We do not focus on standard profiles or corrugated board. Therefore, you can always order from us a non-standard profile or stamping parts. Optimization of orders is the priority of Potoki Group. We always got an orientation for the goals of our partners.

The use of German equipment for rolling metal allows us to expand the products range. Manufacturing of non-standard serial parts for our masters is not a problem. The order will be executed with the maximum quality. Potoki Group's production facilities will ensure speed. We cut metal into strips and sheets.

Attention to detail is a criterion for the quality of large companies. Therefore, the delivery and storage of products is carried out according to specifications. As a result, you get sheet metal, profiles and details corresponding to the NSU.

We are trusted by Ukrainian well-known companies. And thanks to the exact implementation of each order, we became leaders in metal sheet processing in Ukraine and received quality certificates NSU.
