Reinforcing profile for PVC windows

Reinforcing profile for PVC windows provides stiffness and stability of window frames and door assemblies. A steel load-bearing member installed in inner chamber of plastic extrusions increases a performance property of PVC-windows, compensates temperature changes in geometry of plastic or metal, prevents deformation.

Specializing in production of reinforce profile for PVC windows the company POTOKI GROUP covers the requirements of more than half of Ukraine’s leading manufacturers of window constructions. Our enterprise capacities allow us to produce at least 3 million linear meters of reinforce profiles per month. Efficient production and accurate process structuring allow us to ensure the reliability of supply even in times of peak loads and increased customer requirements.

Using the sheet cold-rolled galvanized steel of 1.2 mm to 2 mm in manufacturing the company POTOKI GROUP produces a reinforce profile in a wide range. Our products can be usefully employed in window systems REHAU, KBE, VEKA, WDS, Epsilon, EF -plast.
